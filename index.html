Frequently asked

Everything you need to know about switching to Sparsh. Still unsure? Chat with our specialists.

Do NFC cards work with all phones?

Most modern Android phones and iPhones (iPhone 7 onwards) support NFC. For older models or laptops, your printed QR code and short links offer the same experience.

Can I rewrite the card after printing?

Yes. Your dashboard allows unlimited rewrites. Update destination URLs, swap contact details or pause the card entirely — changes publish instantly.

What data lives on the NFC chip?

Only a secure URL or vCard reference. Personal data lives on your Sparsh landing page with bank-grade encryption, GDPR processes and Indian data residency options.

Is there a battery or maintenance?

No battery needed. The NFC chip powers up from the receiving device. Cards last for years — just avoid bending beyond 30° or exposing to extreme heat.

How fast can you ship large team orders?

Standard cards ship within 3–5 business days. Corporate runs ship in 5–10 days depending on finish and engraving. We offer rush production for events.
