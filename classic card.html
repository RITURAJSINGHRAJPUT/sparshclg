Do phones need an app to read Classic?

No apps required. iPhone 7 and newer plus most Android phones read the NFC chip automatically. We also print a QR code and short link on the back for laptops or legacy devices.

Can I rewrite the card after handing it out?

Absolutely. Your profile link, vCard or microsite can be updated instantly from the Sparsh dashboard. Everyone tapping the card sees the new destination right away.
